Project Rabbit

A redesign of the House Rabbit Resource Network website.

Research

We gathered feedback from 5 user interviews and 9 survey responses. As part of the interview we had users do a usability test on the current HRRN site. After analyzing the results we focused on 3 main areas for improvement: navigation, resources, and donations.

User Persona

Our user persona was modeled after 1st time rabbit owners.

The Problem

First time rabbit owners need a comprehensive, informative, and trusted resource to help guide them through the rabbit adoption process because the current application and resources are diffficult to navigate and understand, leading to potential welfare issues and abandonment of rescued rabbits.

Usability Testing

The oroginal site used a plugin that made looking at rabbits on the site difficult. As well users were frequently confused during the research phase as to what website they were even on.

Testing - Adoption Process

We updated the adoption process by having an adopt me button added under each rabbit profile. We also added a simplified section with the quick and easy steps of the whole process.

Usability Testing - Adoption Process

Last we updated the application to small steps so users don't feel overwhelmed while completing the adoption application.
